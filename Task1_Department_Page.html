Computing and Informatics Department

The Computing and Informatics Department teaches students programming, software development, and computer systems.

Students learn languages like Java, Python, and web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Department of Science Education

The Department of Science Education prepares students for careers in teaching science subjects such as biology, chemistry, and physics.

Students learn educational methodologies alongside deep scientific knowledge to become effective educators.
